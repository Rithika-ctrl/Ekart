1. Overview

Ekart ("we", "our", or "us") is an e-commerce platform developed by Preflex Solutions Pvt. Ltd., with development by Sanjay E, Rithika K, and B Venkatesh. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website, mobile application, or any services offered through Ekart.

By registering, browsing, or purchasing on Ekart, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the platform.

This policy applies to all users — customers, vendors, delivery partners, and guests — accessing Ekart through any channel.

2. Data We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account details: Full name, email address, mobile number, and password (stored AES-encrypted)
  • Delivery addresses: Recipient name, house/street, city, state, and pin code
  • Payment information: Payment method selection (Cash on Delivery or Razorpay); card/UPI details are processed directly by Razorpay and never stored by Ekart
  • Profile photo: Optionally uploaded by the customer
  • Reviews and ratings: Text comments and star ratings you submit for products
  • Refund requests: Reason text and supporting evidence images you upload
  • Vendor information: Business name, contact details, and product listings
  • Delivery partner information: Name, email, mobile, assigned warehouse, and service pin codes

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, products viewed (recently viewed list), search queries, and session activity
  • Order history: Items purchased, order amounts, payment modes, tracking status, and timestamps
  • Device and location data: IP address, browser type, and approximate location (used for India-only geo-restriction)
  • Wishlist and cart data: Products saved, quantities, and timestamps
  • Login activity: Last login timestamp, OAuth2 provider used (Google, Facebook, GitHub)

Information from Third Parties

  • Social login: If you sign in with Google, Facebook, or GitHub, we receive your name, email, and provider ID from that service
  • Geocoding: Approximate address data from mapping APIs used for delivery zone verification

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information solely for legitimate operational and service-improvement purposes:

  • To create and manage your account (customer, vendor, or delivery partner)
  • To process orders, arrange delivery, and send order confirmation emails
  • To send OTPs for account verification, password reset, and delivery confirmation
  • To assign delivery partners and warehouses based on your pin code
  • To display your order history, tracking status, spending analytics, and wishlist
  • To power the AI shopping assistant with your order and cart context (served by Google Gemini API)
  • To notify you when out-of-stock products become available again
  • To send promotional emails only if you have opted in
  • To generate sales and analytics reports for vendors (aggregated, non-identifiable where possible)
  • To detect fraud, enforce geo-restrictions, and maintain platform security
  • To improve our product recommendations, search results, and platform features

We do not sell your personal data to any third party for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Cookies

Ekart uses cookies and browser sessions to maintain your login state and improve your experience. We use the following types:

  • Session cookies: Required to keep you logged in and maintain your shopping cart across page navigation. These expire when you close your browser.
  • Authentication cookies: Used by Spring Security and OAuth2 to remember your login state and social login session.
  • Preference cookies: Store your last viewed products and UI preferences.

We do not currently use third-party advertising or tracking cookies. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling essential cookies may prevent login and cart functionality from working.

For detailed information, see our Cookie Policy.

5. Third-Party Services

Ekart integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Razorpay — Payment gateway for card, UPI, net banking, and wallet transactions. Payment details are handled entirely by Razorpay and are not stored on Ekart's servers. Razorpay Privacy Policy →
  • Cloudinary — Cloud storage for product images, profile photos, and review/refund evidence images. Cloudinary Privacy Policy →
  • Google Gemini API — Powers the AI shopping assistant. Your cart and order context is sent to Gemini to generate responses. No personally identifiable data is retained by the AI service beyond the immediate request.
  • Google / Facebook / GitHub OAuth2 — Used for social login. We receive only your name, email, and provider ID. We do not receive your social media passwords or other account data.
  • TiDB Cloud (Ping CAP) — Cloud-hosted MySQL database (Singapore region) where your account and order data is stored securely.
  • SMTP Email (Gmail) — Used to send transactional emails (OTPs, order confirmations, shipping updates, stock alerts).

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data is shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With vendors: When you place an order, the vendor receives your name, delivery address (city and pin code), and ordered items to fulfill the order.
  • With delivery partners: Assigned delivery boys receive your delivery address pin code and order details to complete delivery.
  • With payment processors: Razorpay receives your payment method details to process transactions.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by Indian law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Platform safety: Fraud investigation or enforcement of our Terms of Service.

7. Data Security

We implement the following technical measures to protect your data:

  • All passwords are encrypted using AES-256 with PBKDF2 key derivation before storage
  • JWT tokens are used for mobile API authentication with 7-day expiry
  • The platform is restricted to India-based access only via IP geo-filtering
  • Database connections use SSL/TLS encryption (TiDB Cloud)
  • Sensitive configuration values (API keys, secrets) are stored in environment variables, not in source code
  • Admin, vendor, customer, and delivery portal access are fully separated with role-based controls

Despite our efforts, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to log out after using shared devices. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, contact us immediately at sanjay@email.com.

8. Your Rights

As a user of Ekart, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information from your account settings page
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated personal data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
  • Portability: Request your order history and account data in a readable format
  • Withdraw consent: Opt out of non-essential communications (promotional emails) at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sanjay@email.com. We will respond within 30 days. Please note that some data may be retained for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes even after an account deletion request.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Active accounts: Retained until you request deletion
  • Order records: Retained for a minimum of 3 years for financial and dispute resolution purposes
  • Review and refund data: Retained for the lifetime of the product listing
  • User activity logs: Last 20 sessions retained; older logs are automatically purged
  • OTP records: Purged within 24 hours of generation

After account deletion, your personal data is removed within 30 days, except for records required by law or active dispute resolution.
